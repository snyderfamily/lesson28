Breakfast Dinner Dessert Bread soup

Breads

White bread

Ingredients

  • 11 cups of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of yeast
  • 6 tablespoons of sugar
  • 6 tablespoons of oil
  • 4 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3 cups of water

Steps

  1. step one; heat up the water until it is warm
  2. pour 4 cups of flour and all the dry ingredients and combine in a bowl
  3. pour the milk and oil into a bowl,slowly mix with the dry Ingredients
  4. add the rest of the flour, stir until thick
  5. knead dough for about ten minuits, flattening it folding it in half rotating it by 90 degrees and repeating
  6. roll the dough into a ball and place in a oiled bowl and leave it covered in a warm place
  7. after letting it rise for thirty minuits put it in a tray and into the oven at 375 degrees until. bake until a utensil comes out clean, thirty minuits to an hour depending on the size of the loaves.
